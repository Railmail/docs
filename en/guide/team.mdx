---
title: "Team & roles"
description: "Invite teammates and manage who can access your organization."
---

Manage who has access to your organization under **Settings → Members**.

## Invite a member

Click **Invite member** and provide:

- **Email** of the person to invite
- **Role** (see below)
- **Project** (for project-scoped access)

They receive an email to join. Pending invitations are listed with their email, role, project, status and expiry. You can **change the role or project** of an invitation, or **revoke** it before it's accepted.

## Roles

| Role | Access |
| --- | --- |
| **Owner** | Full control of the organization. |
| **Admin** | Manage members, projects and settings. |
| **User** | Work within assigned projects. |

## Managing members

For an existing member you can **change their role**, **manage which projects** they can access, or **remove them** from the organization. Member statuses include Active, Pending, Pending verification, Invited, Suspended and Disabled.

## Accepting an invitation

The invited person opens the invitation link and sees who invited them, along with the email and role. They click **Accept invitation** to join, or decline with **Not now**. Expired or invalid links show an *"Invitation unavailable"* message.
