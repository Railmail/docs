title: "Activity feed"
description: "A chronological, real-time feed of everything happening in your project."
---

The **Activity** view (also called the Live Feed) is a chronological timeline of events in your project: campaigns sending, subscribers joining, automations running, and insights from the assistant. It updates in real time, so it's the fastest way to answer "what's happening right now?".

Events are grouped by day (Today, Yesterday, This week, Older) and load continuously as you scroll.

## Filters

Filter the feed with the buttons at the top, or press keys **1**-**5**:

| Key | Filter | Shows |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | All | Everything. |
| 2 | Campaigns | Sent, scheduled, sending, paused, failed, archived, duplicated. |
| 3 | Subscribers | Joined, unsubscribed, bounced, complained, reactivated. |
| 4 | Automations | Activated, completed, paused, failed, created, updated. |
| 5 | AI Insights | Reports ready, suggestions, anomalies, predictions, milestones. |

The feed also surfaces topic and segment changes (created, updated, deleted) and system alerts.

## Keyboard navigation

- **J** / **K**: move down / up through events.
- **Enter**: open the related item (campaign, subscriber, automation).
- **Esc**: clear the focus.

## Acting on events

Many events carry contextual actions: **View report**, **Open campaign**, **View subscriber**, **Details**. Assistant events are actionable too:

- **Suggestions** (for example, a follow-up segment for non-openers): **Approve** to accept or **Dismiss** to discard.
- **Milestones and anomalies** carry badges such as *Above/Below average*, *Growing*, *Declining* or *Critical*, so you can spot trends at a glance.

## Quick stats

A side panel summarizes your project at a glance: active campaigns, active subscribers, average open rate, active automations, topics and segments.
